# Digital Sovereignty Denmark (DSDK)

_**Digital Sovereignty Denmark (DSDK)** is an open, community-driven initiative dedicated to exploring, analysing, and strengthening digital sovereignty in Denmark._

This organisation serves as a collaborative hub for researchers, technologists, policymakers, students, civil society actors, and **anyone** interested in Denmark’s digital autonomy, resilience, and independence.

Our mission is simple:  
➡️ **Build shared knowledge, tools, and ideas that support a sovereign and open digital future for Denmark.**

---

## 🎯 Purpose & Vision

Digital sovereignty touches everything from public-sector IT choices and cloud infrastructure to open source adoption, data control, cybersecurity, and democratic digital governance.

DSDK aims to:

- Map and analyse Denmark’s digital dependencies  
- Explore alternative technologies and open-source options  
- Encourage public collaboration across disciplines and sectors  
- Provide actionable ideas and frameworks for Danish digital autonomy  
- Gather and maintain relevant resources, references, and research  
- Serve as an open platform for experimentation, debate, and knowledge exchange  

We are not affiliated with the Danish government or any political body — this is an **independent, open community initiative**.
